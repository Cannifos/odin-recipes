Daisy 17

A new take on the Whiskey Daisy, Daisy 17

To round out our list of Daisy cocktails, we will be showcasing a Whiskey Daisy.
I picked this recipe up from Justin Sajda, otherwise known as Thirstywhale on social media.
This spin on the classic Whiskey Daisy omits the orange liquer with grenadine, the addition of orange bitters rounds this drink out nicely.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces of overproofed Rye
  • 1 ounce of fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 ounce of Grenadine
  • 1/4 ounce of Simple Syrup
  • 2 dashes of Orange Bitters
  • 2 drops of Saline Solution

Directions

  1. Add lemon juice to shaker.
  2. Add grenadine to shaker.
  3. Add simple syrup to shaker.
  4. Add whiskey to shaker.
  5. Add orange bitters to shaker.
  6. Add saline solution to shaker.
  7. Pour cocktail into a chilled coup.
  8. Express oils of an orange peel on top of drink.
  9. Garnish cocktail with the orange peel.
